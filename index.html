Reflection

This 1st quarter, it was a time of transitioning. I met a lot of new faces, each with distinctive personlity. I also met a lot of new teachers, and they were so good at teaching! I heard that grade 9 is the hardest in Junior Highschool, and I can say, yes it is. Compared to 8th Grade, mas pressuring and mas maraming ginagawa sa Grade 9. It's like, sumeryoso bigla yung school year. Kaya I had a bit of hard time because I had a little trouble in keeping up sa mga gawain. I'm not sure but this school year talaga, I became more prone to cramming. I want to try and fix it next quarter. In ICT, there were a lot of new lessons, because I'm used to the basic lessons like "Microsoft" and basic HTML. But my mind has been opened to a lot of new knowledge in this subject. Nahirapan lang talaga ako sa mga on the spot hands-on because it's very time pressuring and minsan nakakalimutan ko kung ano yung mga tamang codes. Overall, 1st quarter was a little hard for me. But I expect 2nd quarter to be much harder in the future.
